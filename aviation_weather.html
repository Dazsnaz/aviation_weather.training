Lesson 1: The Anatomy of a METAR

A METAR is a snapshot of current weather. Let's look at this example:

METAR KLAX 121853Z 25015G25KT 5SM BR FEW020 18/12 A2992
  • KLAX: The airport (Los Angeles).
  • 25015G25KT: Wind from 250° at 15 knots, gusting to 25.

Quick Quiz

In the report: 21010KT, what does the "210" represent?

The wind speed is 210 knots
The wind is coming FROM 210 degrees
The temperature is 21.0 degrees
